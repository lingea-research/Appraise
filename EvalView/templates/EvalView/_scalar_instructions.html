• Very Good (9–10): Complete meaning transfer. Sounds perfectly natural. (no or minimal proofreading)
  • Good (7–8): Near‑complete transfer, minor inaccuracies. Mostly natural, minor awkwardness. (needs light proofreading)
  • Acceptable (5–6): Main ideas conveyed but with noticeable inaccuracies or omissions. Uneven naturalness, awkward phrasing. (usable only after substantial revision)
  • Borderline (3–4): Partial transfer; frequent misinterpretation or omission that confuses the message. Often unnatural. (requires major rewrite)
  • Not acceptable (1–2): Violation of meaning; large portions mistranslated, missing, or incoherent. (unusable without complete retranslation)
